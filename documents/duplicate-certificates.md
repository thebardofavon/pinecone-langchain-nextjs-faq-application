#### Policy for issuing Certified Official Documents (Semester-wise Grade sheet/ Transcript/ Certificate, etc.) {#iaio .border-bottom draggable="true"}

**A. Category of documents:**

-   [Certified Semester-wise Grade sheet (separate copy for each
    Semester) -- documents will have signature of the current Associate
    Dean in the office.]{#ixizi}
-   [Duplicate Degree Transcript (If original is lost) - The Duplicate
    Degree Transcript will have the signature of the current Associate
    Dean in the office]{#itwx9}
-   [Duplicate Degree Certificate (If original is lost) - The Duplicate
    Degree Certificate will have the signature of the Registrar and the
    Director & Chairperson, Senate of IIITG. In place of the signature
    of the Chairman, Board of Governors, Sd/\-- will be written on the
    Degree Certificate.]{#itt2n}
-   [Certified Degree Transcript (If original is not lost).]{#it3lh}
-   [Certified Degree Certificate (If original is not lost).]{#irjsv}

**B. Procedure:** For issuing any of the above documents, candidate has
to follow the following procedure

-   [Duly filled Application form (hard copy) to the Institute
    (application format is attached as Annexure-I) with following
    documents:]{#i0f6k}
    -   [Self-attested copy of FIR - if original Degree Certificate /
        Transcript is lost.]{#ixikk}
    -   [Original Affidavit prepared by the Notary on a non- judicial
        stamp paper of minimum Rs.10/- (affidavit format attached as
        Annexure -- II) - if original Degree Certificate/ Transcript is
        lost.]{#i5fes}
    -   [Self-attested copy of original Degree Certificate/
        Transcript]{#ic7a5}
    -   [Self-attested copy of PAN card/ Aadhar card/ Driving license/
        Passport/ Voter ID]{#i339v}
    -   [Fee payment receipt (charges and bank details are mentioned
        below in separate section).]{#i7p4t}

-   [Send the filled-up application with all document to the Institute
    address:]{#i7k76}

    **Academic Affairs Section\
    Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati\
    Bongora\
    Guwahati-781015, Assam, India**

**C. Fee payment:**

-   [**Charges:**]{#iiqq4}
    -   [For Duplicate Degree Transcript and Certified Degree
        Certificate = Rs. 1000/-]{#i2zlf}
    -   [For Semester-wise Grade sheet/ other certified documents =
        Rs.300/- per copy]{#ixp2q}
    -   [Postal charges -- Within India: i) Rs.200/- by registered post
        or Speed Post, ii) Rs.500/- by courier. Overseas: i) Rs.1500/-
        by Speed Post/ EMS ii) As per actuals by Courier.]{#irl1c}

-   [**Mode of Payment :** Bank Transfer. Bank details of the
    Institute:]{#idmxb}

    Account Name- Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati\
    Account No. - 34573379424\
    IFSC- SBIN0005242\

**D. Disclaimer:**

-   [Institute will send the receipt of payment of postage to applicant
    for tracking.]{#iv0lq}
-   [Institute will not be responsible for any delay or safe delivery of
    the documents.]{#ir6ej}
