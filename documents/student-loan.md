        : 6000616897. E-mail id : <cb4255@canarabank.com>
    -   Mukesh Kumar Dubey, Sr. Manager, Canara Bank, Retail Asset Hub,
        Guwahati, Contact no : 8967269069. E-mail id :
        <cb6825@canarabank.com>

2.  [Punjab National
    Bank](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/files/pnb_logo_2021.png){target="_blank"}
    provides education loan assistance as a part of PNB Pratibha, for
    premier institutes. The loan requires no collateral security, no
    margin money, no processing fees, no documentation fees and is on a
    15-year repayment basis.

    Please contact the following Bank personals for Loan queries

    Susibrata Dutta\

    Pranjal Dutta\

    loan up to Rs. 7.5 lakhs can be availed without any collateral
    security. For further details, please see the PDF document
    downloaded from the [SBI scholar loan
    webpage](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/files/sbi_loan_2021.pdf){target="_blank"}
    or contact any SBI Branch near you. You may also contact the Branch
