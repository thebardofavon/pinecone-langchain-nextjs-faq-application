#### M.Tech Programme in Computer Science and Engineering (On-Campus) {#m.tech-programme-in-computer-science-and-engineering-on-campus .py-3 .mt-4 .border-top .border-bottom .text-center xss="removed"}

::: {.card .shadow .p-3 .mb-5 .bg-body .rounded}
::: card-header
##### Eligibility
:::

::: card-body
A candidate should have minimum CPI of either **6.0 in a 10 point**
scale or **60% of marks** in the qualifying degree, fulfilling specific
requirements for different categories, as indicated below.

BE/B. Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering/IT/equivalent
program OR M.Sc (Computer Science/Information Technology) OR Graduates
(AMIETE) from the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication
Engineers (IETE) in Computer Science and Engineering OR MCA from a
recognized Institution.

**Relaxation for SC/ST/PwD Candidates**

Eligibility criteria will be relaxed by **5% marks or 0.5 CPI** for
SC/ST/PwD applicants.

**Additional Conditions for Candidates Still to Appear in Qualifying
Examinations**

Candidates still to appear in their qualifying degree examinations may
also apply, provided they appear in all their qualifying degree
examinations and complete all requirements for their degrees
**31-07-2023**. If selected, such candidates shall be admitted
provisionally, and they will have to furnish the results of their
qualifying degree examinations latest by **30-10-2023**. Further, they
must fulfil the minimum requirements of marks/CPI, and other conditions.
Failure to fulfil any of these requirements, shall automatically result
in cancellation of admission.

###### **Reservation of Seats** {#reservation-of-seats .text-info}

Reservation of Seats for SC, ST, OBC (Non-creamy layer), EWS, and PwD
categories. OBC (Non-creamy layer) candidates will have to enclose a
certificate and self-declaration statement as per the prescribed format
provided in the application form. The OBC (NCL) Certificate, issued by
the competent authority, on or after 01-06-2022, shall only be
acceptable. The seat matrix (including Regular and Online) is as
follows.

  GE   GE-PwD   SC   SC-PwD   ST   ST-PwD   OBC   OBC-PwD   EWS   Total
  ---- -------- ---- -------- ---- -------- ----- --------- ----- -------
  29   1        9    0        5    0        15    1         2     62
