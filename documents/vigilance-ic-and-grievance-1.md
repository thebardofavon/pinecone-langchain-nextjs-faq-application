#### VIGILANCE {#vigilance .text-center}

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati, adheres to the
rules and guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission, towards making
the institute a righteous and corruption-free organization.

**Vigilance related Complaints** : For complaints related to
mal-practices and corruption or other illegal activities in the
organization, complaints may be lodged to the following link :
[https://portal.cvc.gov.in/](https://portal.cvc.gov.in/){target="_blank"}

Process of lodging complaints :

-   Please click on the citizen login link in the home web page of the
    aforementioned link. After registering, provide complete details of
    the complaints, viz. nature of complaint, the individual(s) against
    whom complaint is lodged, contact details of the complainant.
-   Provision for whistle blower complaints : In cases where the
    complainants wish to be discrete, under Public Interest Disclosure
    and Protection of Informer (PIDPI), he/she can lodge complaint.
    However, in this case complaints must be sent only by post, to CVC.
    (CENTRAL VIGILANCE COMMISSION,Satarkta Bhavan , Block-A, GPO Complex
    , INA, New Delhi - 110 023). In these posts, the complainant should
    refrain from mentioning his/her name and contact details in the
    envelope. These details should be mentioned in the body of the
    complaint letter.

For further details of the complaints handling policy, please go through
the following document: [Click
Here](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/files/CVC_complaint_handling_policy.pdf){target="_blank"}

**Redressal of Complaints**: After receiving any complaint, the CVC will
contact CVO of the organization for investigation and resolution. In
such cases, the complainants will co-operate with the CVO by providing
all supporting documents.

For further information on any vigilance matters, one may go through the
latest Vigilance Manual by CVC [Click
Here](https://www.iiitg.ac.in/uploads/files/ENGLISH_Vigilance_Manual_2021.pdf){target="_blank"}

##### GRIEVANCE {#grievance .text-center}

For the complaints related to deficiency in delivery of goods and/ or
services by the organization, including service, materials, facilities
and personnel matters, complaints may be sent to Chairman, Grievance
Cell, IIIT Guwahati (<grievance@iiitg.ac.in>). If complainants fail to
get redress to grievances from IIIT Guwahati, they can approach
Directorate of Pubic Grievances (DPG) at
[https://dpg.gov.in](https://dpg.gov.in/default.aspx){target="_blank"}
for redress of their grievances.

###### INTERNAL COMMITTEE {#internal-committee .text-center}

The Internal Committee of the institute formerly known as the Internal
Complaints Committee has been constituted in compliance of the Sexual
Harassment of Women at workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal
Act 2013 of the Ministry of Law and Justice, Govt of India.

The Internal Committee will pursue matters specifically related to
sexual harassment of women at the workplace and make recommendations and
report to the Director. "Sexual harassment" includes all unwelcome
sexually determined behaviour (whether directly or by implication), as
defined by relevant rules/Acts of Govt of India and Guidelines and Court
Orders which can be deemed detrimental or discriminatory to a women's
dignity, safety, well-being.

The committee will admit and examine complaints received from aggrieved
women, who alleged to have been subjected to any act of sexual
harassment at the workplace. Complaints can be made by employees of the
Institute, students of the Institute, persons engaged to work in the
campus by various external agencies.

In case of any complaints, the complainant may send a complaint to the
email ic@iiitg.ac.in or contact the member secretary at 7002446793.
