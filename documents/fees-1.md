# Fees

:::

::: {role="main"}
::: {#ibfo .container}
#### M.Tech. Programme in Electronics and Communication Engineering {#ikzy .heading .border-bottom .pb-2 .mt-4}

Specializations:

-   Communication and Signal Processing \[Offered both in Online/Remote
    Mode and On-campus Mode\]
-   VLSI and Embedded System \[Offered only in On-campus Mode\])

::: {#iaat7 .card}
::: {#iv58h .card-body}
##### [Fees]{xss="removed"} {#i1uo4}

[These are self-financed courses and all students have to pay the full
fees as shown below.]{xss="removed"}

##### [[ON-CAMPUS Mode]{xss="removed"}]{xss="removed"} {#ijooi .py-2}

::: {#is5lc}
  Serial No.                                                 Items                      Periodicity             Amount (in Rs.)
  ---------------------------------------------------------- -------------------------- ----------------------- -----------------
  1                                                          Tuition Fee                Per Semester            75,000
  2                                                          Institute Caution Money    One Time (Refundable)   5,000
  3                                                          Hostel Rent                Every six months        12,500
  4                                                          Mess Charges (Estimated)   Every Semester          16,000
  GRAND TOTAL (1 to 4) to be paid on the time of admission                                                      1,08,000
:::

##### [[ONLINE Mode]{xss="removed"}]{xss="removed"} {#idmwt}

[Payment of Fees by M.Tech students in online mode is to be on a per
credit basis. In order to allow students to take up the courses in the
programmes at their own place, fees will be charged on a per credit
completion basis every semester.]{xss="removed"}

[Fees for the Online M.Tech ECE (Communication and Signal Processing)
programme :]{xss="removed"}

[Charge per Credit: Rs. 2500.]{xss="removed"}

[A student taking the normal load will pay fees as
follows:]{xss="removed"}

::: {#i20eh}
  Serial No.   Semester       Number of Credits   Fees to be paid ( Rs.)
  ------------ -------------- ------------------- ------------------------
  1            Monsoon 2022   33                  82,500
  2            Winter 2023    27                  67,500
  3            Monsoon 2023   30                  75,000
  4            Winter 2024    30                  75,000
  Total                       120                 3,00,000
:::

# [NOTE:]{xss="removed"} {#id0gxy .heading .h5}

::: {#i0c9ya .section .bdg-sect}
[Students taking more than the minimum number of credits will not be
charged for the extra credits.\
]{xss="removed"}[If a student registers for 30 credits, he has to pay
fees of Rs. 75,000. Subsequently, if he/she drops a course of 6 credits,
Rs. 15,000 will be carried forward to the next semester. If a student
fails in a course, he/she will be charged for the course, but if he/she
repeats the course or gives a supplementary examination, there will be
no extra charge.\
]{xss="removed"}[So, a student has to pay fees of Rs. 3,00,000 for the
programme, irrespective of the time taken to complete, or the number of
extra credits taken beyond the specified 120 credits.]{xss="removed"}
:::
