### IIIT Guwahati Counselling Services

IIIT Guwahati provides free on-campus and online counselling to students
and members of faculty and staff and their families. Our counselling
partner is YourDost Health Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Counselling services include individual consultation, group counselling,
interventions during crisis, etc. Additionally, online and on-campus
workshops are held on topics related to well-being of students and
members of faculty and staff, such as living harmoniously on campus,
time management, living an influence free life and barefoot counselling,
to name a few.

#### On-campus services

Individual consultations are provided by experienced clinical
psychologists every week. The sessions are on every Friday from 3 pm to
5 pm by Ms. Amrita Agarwal and every Saturday from 10 am to 1 pm by Ms.
Bandita Das at Room No.2022, Tower 2, Faculty Quarters, IIIT Guwahati.
Those wishing to avail counselling services may walk in or book a prior
appointment through the [YourDost
portal](https://yourdost.com/){target="_blank"} .

#### Online services

Individual consultations are provided round-the-clock every day of the
year through a dedicated web based platform. Users of the service can
chat with an expert or talk over an audio or video call. The details on
how to access these services are provided under Accessing online
services .

For both on-campus and online services, the identity of the person
availing the services will not be disclosed and the details of the
counselling services sought will be confidential.
