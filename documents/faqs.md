# FAQs

::: accordion-item
## [1. How well is Guwahati connected with the rest of the country?]{.accordion-button bs-toggle="collapse" bs-target="#acco1" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="acco1"} {#headingOne .accordion-header}

::: {#acco1 .accordion-collapse .collapse .show aria-labelledby="headingOne" bs-parent="#accordion"}
::: accordion-body
Guwahati, which is the gateway for North East India, is well connected
by air, rail and road with the rest of the country. There are direct air
services from/to Bangalore, Mumbai, New Delhi and Kolkata with
connectivity to all major cities in the country and direct train
services from/to all the metropolises and other major cities.
:::
:::
:::

::: accordion-item
## [2. How to reach the institute?]{.accordion-button .collapsed bs-toggle="collapse" bs-target="#acco2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="acco2"} {#headingTwo .accordion-header}

::: {#acco2 .accordion-collapse .collapse aria-labelledby="headingTwo" bs-parent="#accordion"}
::: accordion-body
The institute is located at Bongora, which is around 4 km from the
Guwahati Airport. Pre-paid taxis are available in the Airport. Taxis are
available for transport within the city and can be booked online (Prime
cabs, Swiss cabs, Ola, Uber, etc.). Auto-rickshaws are available but
there are no meters and fares have to be negotiated beforehand. There is
also an extensive network of city buses.
:::
:::
:::

::: accordion-item
## [3. When does the placement session begin and for how long does it continue?]{.accordion-button .collapsed bs-toggle="collapse" bs-target="#acco3" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="acco3"} {#headingThree .accordion-header}

::: {#acco3 .accordion-collapse .collapse aria-labelledby="headingThree" bs-parent="#accordion"}
::: accordion-body
The placement starts in July 2022. Companies are encouraged to visit the
campus before the placement session begins to give Pre-Placement Talks
to our students and introduce them to the job profiles and job culture
of their company. This does have a direct bearing on the interest shown
by students when the company visits the campus later for placements.
:::
:::
:::

::: accordion-item
## [4. We are under the impression that we can recruit students only if their discipline is related our Industry. Is this so?]{.accordion-button .collapsed bs-toggle="collapse" bs-target="#acco4" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="acco4"} {#headingThree .accordion-header}

::: {#acco4 .accordion-collapse .collapse aria-labelledby="headingThree" bs-parent="#accordion"}
::: accordion-body
No, the institute imposes no restrictions in this regard. Companies can
opt to recruit students from any department or discipline.
:::
:::
:::
